Sevika Sangam

              Mar Thoma Suvishesha Sevika Sangham is the women's organization of The Mar Thoma Syrian Church which celebrated 100 years of its existence in 2019, established with the purpose of providing a platform for Marthomite women (above the age of 18) to play a significant role in the evangelistic and charitable mission of the church. A place of reflection, bonding and fellowship, for the women of our church, the St. Thomas Mar Thoma Sevika Sangham channelizes the individual strengths and capabilities of our women into one cohesive and unstoppable force. The Sevikas, a reckoning force involves in critical yet neglected responsibilities of attending to the needy with touch of love and care and raising funds within to sponsor especially children and physically and medically challenged people of the society. May the Lord take and use the Sevikas for the extension of the Kingdom of God in the vicinity of Palakkad.
